Enumerator

No async operation in progress.

Queued for asynchronous loading.

ASYNC_LOADING 

In progress of calling BeginLoad() in a worker thread.

ASYNC_SUCCESS 

BeginLoad() succeeded. EndLoad() can be called in the main thread.

ASYNC_FAIL 

BeginLoad() failed.
